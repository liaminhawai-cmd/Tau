What it is

Two tripods on a round board marked with rings and arcs. On your turn you pin one foot and swing the piece around it. Cross a line and you have to stop. The whole game is leverage: get the other piece near the edge at a bad angle, then push it off, while keeping your own three points of contact safe. Anyone who wrestles or trains judo reads it instantly, it is kuzushi with a scoreboard.

  • Two players, a few minutes a game, no luck.
  • Steel board, magnetic pieces, they hold their line like the real thing.
  • The same rules as the digital version, which you can play right now to learn it.

## CONCEPTUAL CONCLUSION (settled — more scanning won't move it)
- The entire "game mat" category is GRIP-FIRST and off-spec. Mats are engineered so components
  DON'T slide (rubber/neoprene/cloth backing, "just enough grip"). Tau's big model needs the
  opposite. Stop searching "game mat"; search the material directly (smooth vinyl, slick PVC,
  acrylic, HDPE). QinPrinting (AU) confirmed off-spec: rubber-backed printed cloth, sold only as
  full game sets. StandoutMats / Contrado = non-slip neoprene, off-spec.
- Printable self-adhesive vinyl is a SKIN, not a surface. It's made to hold ink (often matte/
  textured face), needs bonding to a rigid slick substrate. Solves printing, not sliding.
- Line-only vs full colour: NO cost difference on digital/UV print (cost = media + machine time).
  Fewer colours only saves money in screen printing at volume. So "line-art" is not a budget
  option here — its only real role is the draw-your-own tier (buyer constructs the board), a
  different axis from cost.

## THE POSTAGE FINDING (the decider — it reshaped the lanes)
Live AusPost check (Sydney->Melbourne proxy):
- Rolled TUBE 105x15x15cm, 1.5kg = Parcel Post AUD 26.30 (3d) / Express AUD 40.50 (1d).
- Rigid FLAT PANEL ~105x105x5cm, 4kg = Parcel Post AUD 41.10 (3d) / Express AUD 100.90 (1d).
- AusPost caps: max length 105cm, max volume 0.25 m^3. A true 1 m flat board is AT/OVER the limit
  once packed.
Consequence: a rigid flat metre panel barely ships (or doesn't) and is 3-4x the tube cost when it
does. Caps are about dimensions not distance, so this holds for any origin (incl. Asia fulfilment);
only the dollar figures change. => Rigid-at-1m is inherently a LOCAL-PICKUP product. Rollable is the
only shape that MAILS at this size.

## THE THREE SLICK LANES (this is the real structure — two products for two situations)
1. RIGID-LOCAL: acrylic / slick plastic, laser-cut to a 1 m circle, collected or short-haul local
   delivery. Metre size is fine because it never enters the post. NOT a mailable national product.
2. ROLLABLE-MAILABLE: smooth flexible surface (smooth vinyl, slick PVC sheet, vinyl flooring),
   ships in a tube for ~1/4 the rigid cost. The only shape that mails at 1 m.
3. DRAW-YOUR-OWN (blank slick stock): blank smooth mat + a few numbered anchor dots + string +
   marker; buyer rules up the compass/straightedge board themselves. Cheapest, most on-brand,
   dodges chalk-on-bitumen. Lean dry-erase/whiteboard-surface + dry-erase markers for reusable.
